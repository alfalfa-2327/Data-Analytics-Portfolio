## Projects

### Project 1 — Best Smartphones Under ₹30,000 in India
Analysed 9 popular smartphones in the ₹23,000–₹28,000 price range.
Collected data from Flipkart on price, battery, camera, processor, and ratings.
Used Excel formulas and charts to find key insights.
Used AI (Claude) to generate business recommendations.

Key findings:
- Best overall value: iQOO Z10 5G (₹24,999) — largest battery at 7,300 mAh
- Highest rated: Nothing Phone 2a (4.5/5 stars)
- Fastest charging: OnePlus Nord CE4 (100W)

Files included:
- Phone_Analysis_Under_30k.xlsx — data, formulas, charts
- Phone_Analysis_Report.pdf — full written report
- Chart1_Ratings.png — phone ratings comparison chart
- Chart2_Battery.png — battery capacity comparison chart
